Biology

Biology is the scientific study of life and living organisms. It is a broad field that examines everything from the microscopic molecules that make up cells to the complex interactions within entire ecosystems. By studying biology, we seek to understand how organisms are structured, how they function, how they grow, how they evolve, and how they interact with their environment.
